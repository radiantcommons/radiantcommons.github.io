the mission

Radiant Commons is a non-stock corporation focused on technologies that empower users to consent to when and how their information is used.

Radiant focuses on four main activities:

  • We support teams on the creation of ecosystem tooling and middleware to empower universal and permissionless interaction between protocols.
  • We contribute upstream to vision-aligned technological ecosystems through an in-house tech team.
  • We help communities maintain open-source technologies by building, managing and researching coordination mechanisms while actively moderating and participating in governing structures.
  • Finally, we incubate teams building consentful technologies with the goal of spinning them out into independent entities.
